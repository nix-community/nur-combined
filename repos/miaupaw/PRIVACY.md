# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 22, 2026

At Instant Eyedropper Reborn (IE-R), we believe that your digital intent is your private business. Our philosophy is rooted in the "No-Bloat, No-Tracking" manifesto.

### 1. Zero Data Collection
IE-R **does not collect, store, or transmit** any personal data, usage statistics, telemetry, or crash reports. 
- No unique identifiers.
- No tracking cookies.
- No "phone home" features.

### 2. Local-First Processing
All color sampling and processing happen entirely on your machine. The screen capture data never leaves your system's volatile memory and is destroyed immediately after the color is picked.

### 3. Network Communication
IE-R is designed to function entirely offline. The only instance of network communication is:
- **User-Initiated:** When you explicitly click a link in the "About" window (e.g., to visit our website).

### 4. Clipboard Data
IE-R interacts with your system clipboard only to provide the color data you have requested. It does not monitor or store your clipboard history.

### 5. Compliance
Since we do not collect any personal information, IE-R is naturally compliant with **GDPR**, **CCPA**, and other global privacy regulations by design (Privacy by Default).
